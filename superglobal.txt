
PHP Global VAriables - Superglobals

Superglobals were introduced in PHP 4.1.0, and are built-in variables that are always available in all scopes.

Several prededined varablse in PHP are 'superglobals', which means that they are always accessible, regardless of scope - and 
you can access them from any function, class or file without having to do anything special.

The PHP superglobal variables are:

- $_GLOBLAS
- $_SERVER
- $_POST
- $_REQUEST
- $_GET
- $_FILES
- $_ENV
- $_COOKIE
- $_SESSION
